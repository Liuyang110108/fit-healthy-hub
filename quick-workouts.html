Quick Home Workouts Without Equipment

No gym? No gear? No problem. These quick home workouts without equipment will get you sweating in just 15 minutes. Perfect for beginners or anyone short on time.

Benefits of Bodyweight Exercises

Using your own body weight builds strength, burns calories, and improves flexibility—all without costly gear. Plus, you can do them anywhere in your home.

15-Minute Workout Plan

Repeat this circuit 3 times, resting 30 seconds between exercises:

1. Push-Ups (1 min)

Standard or on knees—works chest, arms, and core. Aim for 10-15 reps.

2. Squats (1 min)

Feet shoulder-width apart, lower until thighs are parallel to the floor. Targets legs and glutes.

3. Plank (1 min)

Hold a straight line from head to heels. Strengthens your entire core.

4. Jumping Jacks (1 min)

Classic cardio to spike your heart rate. Go as fast as you can!

5. Mountain Climbers (1 min)

In plank position, bring knees to chest rapidly. Great for abs and endurance.

Pro Tips

Warm up with light stretching, stay hydrated, and increase reps as you get stronger. No equipment needed—just commitment.

Get Moving Today

These quick home workouts without equipment prove fitness is accessible to all. Start now and build a stronger you!
