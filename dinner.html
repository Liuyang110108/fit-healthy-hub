Healthy Dinner Recipes Under 500 Calories

Craving tasty dinners without the guilt? These healthy dinner recipes under 500 calories are simple, delicious, and weight-loss friendly.

Why Low-Calorie Dinners?

Light dinners help you stay in a calorie deficit while satisfying your hunger—a win for health goals.

Top Recipes

1. Grilled Chicken Salad (350 kcal)

4 oz chicken breast, mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, balsamic drizzle. 15 minutes to make.

2. Zucchini Noodles with Shrimp (400 kcal)

Spiralize 1 zucchini, sauté with 6 shrimp and garlic. Low-carb delight.

3. Veggie Stir-Fry (450 kcal)

Broccoli, bell peppers, tofu, and soy sauce over ½ cup brown rice. Quick and filling.

Cooking Tips

Use olive oil sparingly, load up on veggies, and measure portions. Healthy dinners under 500 calories are easier than you think.

Eat Well Tonight

These recipes keep you on track without sacrificing flavor. Try one tonight!
