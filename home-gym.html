Home Gym Setup on a Budget

Want a fitness space without spending a fortune? This home gym setup on a budget guide proves you can get fit for under $200.

Why a Budget Home Gym?

No membership fees, no commute—just effective workouts at home. Start small, save big.

Essentials Under $200

1. Resistance Bands ($20)

Lightweight, versatile—great for strength and stretching.

2. Jump Rope ($10)

Cheap cardio powerhouse. Burns calories fast.

3. Yoga Mat ($30)

Comfort for floor exercises—doubles as a workout space.

4. Adjustable Dumbbells ($100)

5-25 lbs range. Covers all lifting needs.

5. Storage Bin ($20)

Keeps gear tidy in a corner.

Setup Tips

Pick a 6x6 ft spot, shop sales, and add gear over time. A home gym on a budget grows with you.

Fitness, Affordably

For $200, your home gym is ready. Start sweating without breaking the bank!
