Beginner Strength Training Routine

New to fitness? This beginner strength training routine builds muscle and confidence at home—no gym required. Start strong with these simple moves.

Why Strength Training?

Lifting your body weight boosts metabolism, strengthens bones, and tones muscles. It’s perfect for beginners looking to level up.

20-Minute Routine

Do 3 rounds, 10-12 reps each, resting 1 minute between exercises:

1. Bodyweight Squats

Stand tall, lower hips to knee level, then rise. Targets legs and glutes.

2. Push-Ups (Knee Option)

Hands under shoulders, lower chest to floor. Builds arms and chest.

3. Plank Hold (30 sec)

Keep a straight line from head to heels. Strengthens core.

4. Lunges

Step forward, lower back knee, alternate legs. Works balance and thighs.

5. Wall Sit (30 sec)

Lean against a wall, thighs parallel to floor. Tones legs fast.

Beginner Tips

Focus on form over speed, start with 2 sessions a week, and stretch after. You’ll feel stronger in no time.

Build Your Foundation

This beginner strength training routine is your first step to a fitter you. Stick with it and watch progress happen!
